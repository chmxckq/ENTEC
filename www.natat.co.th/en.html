Senior Account Manager(Sales)

Responsibilities

• Present, promote and sell products/services for IT Solutions (Servers, Storages, Network etc.)

• Perform cost-benefit and needs analysis of existing/potential customers to meet their needs

• Make accurate, rapid cost calculations and providing customers with quotations

• Establish, develop, and maintain business and customer relationships

• Corporate with government customers TOR and manage e-bidding

• Expedite the resolution of customer problems and complaints to maximize satisfaction

• Coordinate sales effort with team members, PM, and other departments

• Control and report Budget, Actual and analyze situations.

• Set and monitor sales targets as well as support team’s target

• Identify potential new customers and set approach strategies

 

Qualification

• At least 3 years experiences in Account Manager (Sales) for IT Solutions business.

• Able to lead Sales team members under same group accounts.

• Understand process and associated documentation for government’s bidding will be advantages.

• Understand process and associated documentation for government’s bidding will be advantages.

System Engineer (Required VMWare Cloud Foundation))

Responsibilities:

 

•Work closely with project management teams, business partners, or external teams to oversee installation work for customers,

•Focusing on VMWare Cloud Foundation (CVF) installation (CVF skill is advantage) and IT solutions

•Responsible for overseeing technical installation to ensure successful work based on the specified solutions that have proposed to customers.

•Plan and execute installations accurately to meet customer requirements.

•Coordinate with relevant teams, both internal and external, to ensure successful installation and timely delivery to customers.

•Ability to install, configure, maintain, and troubleshoot various technical aspects of the systems we offer to customers.

•Evaluate work and provide recommendations or suggestions when issues arise within a particular project.

•Provide technical advice to customers.

•Collect and compile technical information for each solution to be included in critical documents.

•Ability to create system structure diagrams, operation documents, and assess the impact on the planned system.

 

Requirements:

 

•Thai nationality.

•Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, Telecommunications Engineering, Information Technology (MIS), or related fields.

•3 years or more of experience in IT system installation, preferably as a System Engineer.

•Experience in the foundational structure of VMWare Cloud Foundation IT systems will be considered a plus.

•Experience with Dell, Dell EMC, VMware products, and network security products will be considered a plus.

•Familiarity with working in government structures, such as preparing documents and information for each project.

•Good personal presentation, communication, negotiation skills, organizational skills, and good interpersonal skills.

•Ability to work under pressure, both independently and in a team.

•Ability to work overtime.

•Self-motivated and eager to learn new technologies.

Secretary to CEO

﻿﻿Responsibilities

  • Provides high-level administrative support and assistance to the Chief Executive Office (CEO) & management team
  • Performs administrative tasks including drafting letters, memos, invoices, reports, and other documents
  • Arranges travel and accommodations for executives.
  • Ensure the professionals they assist have all of the resources they need.
  • Managing multiple or complex calendars for meetings, travel and personal commitments
  • Sitting in on meetings to take minute and creating memos, reports and agendas as needed


Requirements

  • Age preferably 25-32 
  • Able to communicate in English will be advantages
  • Excellent time management and organization skills, especially the ability to prioritize and multitask
  • Effective and professional communication abilities, including being able to communicate with all levels of employees, vendors and clients or guests
  • Professional writing capabilities, including emails, memos, letters and other industry-related reports, documents and correspondence
  • Great customer service and interpersonal skills
  • Have own car
