Images of the Ambiance at the Dell Forum 2023
21 Sep 2023

Photos capturing the vibrant atmosphere at the Dell Forum 2023, held at the prestigious Eastin Grand Phayathai on September 21, 2023, have been made available for viewing. NAT Absolute Technologies PCL actively participated in this event by setting up a dedicated presentation booth. The highlight of our presentation revolved around the cutting-edge technological innovation known as Cyber Recovery as a Service (CR aaS). In addition to sharing valuable information, an engaging question-and-answer game was conducted to foster dialogue and interaction. NAT Absolute Technologies Public Company Limited submits a filing for the sale of ordinary shares
8 Aug 2023

NAT Absolute Technologies Public Company Limited, or NAT, has submitted its initial public offering (IPO) for common shares to the public. For this IPO, the company has appointed Asset Pro Management Limited as its financial advisor. Investors and interested parties can find more details in the company's prospectus, available on the website of the Office of the Securities and Exchange Commission (www.sec.or.th), and the general information about the company at www.natat.co.th. Additionally, information can be accessed on the Stock Exchange of Thailand's website at www.set.or.th.
