MTECH Excellence Business Transformation
7 Jul 2023

Partner Connect 2023, 20th Anniversary: Golden Era, The Gala night witnessed the prestigious recognition of Mr. Pheemaphol Munchukangwan, Assistant Director of Engineering at NAT Absolute Technologies PCL, as the recipient of the MTECH Excellence Business Transformation 2022 award. This honor was bestowed upon him during the Partner Connect 2023, 20th Anniversary: Golden Era, The Gala night held at The Athenee Hotel, a Luxury Collection Hotel in Bangkok. The award was presented by M Technologies (Thailand) Ltd.

SEA Promising Cloud Partner of the Year
18 May 2023

At the Genesys APAC Partner Conference 2023, held at the JW Marriott Phuket Resort & Spa, was captured in photos. Mr. Pheemaphol Munchukangwan, Assistant Director of Business Development and Engineering Supervisor (AVP Engineering) at Nat Consulting Co., Ltd., received the award for SEA Promising Cloud Partner of the Year.
