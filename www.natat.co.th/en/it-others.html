Cabling System

We provide Cabling Services, LAN, Electrical and Telephone System Indoor and Outdoor. Furthermore, the Installation Service Network / Power / Telephone / Fiber Optic Signal cabling system is considered as the basic system for communicating signals between various devices in the computer system, so planning the signal cabling system is the priority that the company must consider. The company has a team of experts who provide advice, planning, design, as well as project control to ensure that all customers' work is in accordance with the standards.

Interior contractor

The company offers comprehensive interior decoration and design services tailored to meet the specific requirements of the clients. The services encompass a range of aspects including wall works, ceilings, floors, as well as mechanical and electrical installations (M&E). Here's an outline of the provided services:

  • Custom Interior Renovation: Providing services for interior renovation based on the preferences and requirements of the client. This includes customization of walls, ceilings, and floors to align with the desired aesthetic and functionality.
  • M&E Works: Incorporating Mechanical and Electrical (M&E) works into the interior design. This involves planning, installation, and integration of various systems within the building to ensure optimal functionality and efficiency.
  • System Installation: Designing, planning, and installing internal systems within the building. This encompasses a variety of systems to enhance the overall operation and comfort of the space.
  • Office Design and Relocation: Offering design solutions and facilitating office relocations. This involves planning and executing the setup of the workspace, ensuring smooth transitions during office moves or relocations.
  • Client-Centric Approach: Understanding and accommodating client needs by actively listening to their requirements, considering work patterns, evaluating budgetary constraints, and suggesting adjustments to align with the client's preferences.
  • Site Assessment: Conducting on-site assessments to accurately measure the space, evaluate existing systems, and plan the installation of furnishings and equipment accordingly.
  • Furniture Delivery and Installation: Providing seamless delivery and installation of office furniture, ensuring a functional and aesthetically pleasing workspace.
  • Warranty and Guarantee: Offering warranties and guarantees for the implemented solutions, as agreed upon during the initial discussions and planning phases.

The objective is to deliver tailored interior design and renovation solutions that not only meet the client's expectations but also enhance the functionality and visual appeal of the space while considering cost-effectiveness and durability.
