The Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) plans to list its initial public offering (IPO) of 92 million shares on the MAI.
10 Nov 2023

 Mr. Somsak Sirichaiyarnimit, Chairman and CEO of Asset Pro Management Company Limited or APM, as the financial advisor of Net Absolute Technologies Public Company Limited or NAT, revealed that the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) has started the process of listing the securities offering information or filing for the initial public offering (IPO) of NAT's common shares on November 10, 2023, which has been completed.

 Ms. Maturos Saraneeyatham, Managing Director of Asset Pro Management Company Limited (APM), as the financial advisor, stated that currently, NAT has a registered capital of 164 million baht with a par value of 0.50 baht per share. The paid-up capital is 118 million baht. NAT plans to offer 92 million common shares, equivalent to 28.05% of the total issued and sold ordinary shares of the company, in its first IPO. After this IPO, all common shares will be listed on the Market for Alternative Investment (mai) under the Technology (TECH) sector.

 Mr. Sutee Apichonratanakorn, Chairman and CEO of NAT Absolute Technologies Public Company Limited (NAT), said that the listing on the Market for Alternative Investment (mai) is a significant step for the company in strengthening its financial capabilities and expanding its business opportunities in various future projects that require the development of information technology and communication systems.

 NAT is an expert in Infratech, specializing in the construction of information and communication technology infrastructure systems. The company provides comprehensive technology services to leading organizations in the country. The services are divided into two groups: 1. Information and Communication Technology System Integration, which includes the sale, installation, and contracting of related systems, and 2. Other services related to information and communication technology, such as IT Outsourcing, Cabling Systems, Mechanical and Electrical (M&E) systems, and rental of document printers, photocopiers, and laptops.

 In the period from 2020 to the 6th month of 2023, the company's customer base consisted of approximately 66.54-89.34% from the public sector and approximately 10.66-33.46% from the private sector, in terms of sales and service revenue. The company aims to continue its continuous growth by implementing plans to utilize leading technology from global business partners to develop the best solutions and services that meet customer needs and to support customers in preparing for the digital technology era. The company aims to expand its customer base in both the public and private sectors, including hospital groups and energy groups. Additionally, the company recognizes the importance of cybersecurity technology and is prepared to develop systems to provide services to customers, including consulting, design, procurement, installation, and related network security systems to ensure secure data storage, prevent complex cyber-attacks, and reduce risks to the business information technology system.

 For the past 3 years (2020-2022), the company's total revenue was 492.64 million baht, 451.36 million baht, and 1,093.23 million baht, respectively. The net profit was 56.78 million baht, 26.68 million baht, and 100.62 million baht, respectively. In the first 6 months of 2023, the total revenue was 788.25 million baht, and the net profit was 65.48 million baht.
