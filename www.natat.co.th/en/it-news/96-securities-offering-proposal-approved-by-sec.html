Securities Offering Proposal Approved by SEC
16 Nov 2023

NAT Advanced Solutions Technologies Co., Ltd. (NAT), a public company, has been approved for its securities offering by the Office of the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). NAT is preparing to launch an Initial Public Offering (IPO) and be listed on the Market for Alternative Investment (MAI). Investors and interested parties can find more details in the company's prospectus at the SEC's website, www.sec.or.th, and general information about the company at www.natat.co.th and the Stock Exchange of Thailand's website, www.set.or.th.

NAT X NT Sales Workshop
"Open House, Open Heart, Open to New Things for NT" Power of P: Join Us, Experience a world of endless possibilities. NAT Absolute Technologies PCL and National Telecom Public Company Limited (NT) are jointly organizing an academic seminar and workshop titled "Open House, Open Heart, Open to New Things for NT" Power of P: Join Us, Experience a world of endless possibilities. This event is made possible through collaboration with our business partners, including DELL Technologies, Radware, Fortinet, Genesys, and Group-IB. The topics of discussion include "Opening Communication Solutions with Intelligent Cloud PBX and Cloud Contact Center" followed by an engaging discussion on "Shining a Light on New Threats with Real-life Experiences" along with a presentation on "Exploring New Perspectives with Design Thinking" and a workshop on Monday, July 10, 2566, at the Phra Prom Conference Room, Asawin Grand Convention Hotel, Bangkok.
