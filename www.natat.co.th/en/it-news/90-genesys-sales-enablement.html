Genesys Sales Enablement
7 Nov 2023

Genesys Cloud CX is a comprehensive platform with tools to deliver the best Customer Experience. It is an All-in-one Omnichannel Platform that combines telephone and digital communication channels. Employees can communicate with customers through various channels such as phone, chat, messaging, email, or social media, all in one interface. It also allows storing customer data and past transactions, enabling employees to quickly understand and comprehend customers' background. This makes it easier to deliver the best customer experience.

Key communication features include AI & Bots, Callbacks, Chat, Email, Inbound & Outbound Voice, IVR, Messaging Apps, Predictive Routing, Social Media, Text/SMS, and Workforce Optimization. Genesys Cloud CX seamlessly integrates with various systems such as CRM, HR management, internal communication, and AI systems. It offers more than 350 Pre-built Customer Experience Apps/Integrations on the AppFoundry Marketplace, including integration options with Google, Microsoft, Oracle, Salesforce, SugarCRM, or Zoom. Additionally, Genesys provides APIs and SDKs for further integration with other organizational systems.
