Company History

   NAT Absolute Technologies Public Company Limited (formerly Nat Consulting Company Limited) was established in 2004, with its primary focus on Information Technology and Telecommunication business. The company provides consulting services for Infrastructure, Network, LAN and computer systems, and offers Maintenance Service Agreements (MA). The expertise for conducting this business comes from the founders of the company, who graduated in the field of Information Technology.

After 10+ years of experience in 2017, the Company has expanded its business into IT Outsourcing by sending IT experts to be stationed at various departments at customer sites (Onsite Support), and at the same time, the Company has expanded into the Information and Communication Technology Business and provides other services related to Information and Communication Technology systems comprehensively. This is a full entry into the business of System Integrator or "SI".

From confident business operations, growth in sales and expertise of personnel, the company is trusted by leading product and service partners such as moving from Gold, Platinum into the highest level which is TITANIUM PARTNER with DELL TECHNOLOGIES in 2021 and continues until now, business partner for sales of products and services from VMWARE (Thailand) Co., Ltd., Cloud Reseller from Genesys, Reseller from Nokia (Thailand), etc.

As a result of its continuous growth and sustainable business operations, the Company has gained the trust of customers from government agencies, private sectors, as well as industry sectors at the national level. The total project value is more than 1,000 million Baht. In 2021 to present, the Company has set a target to transform from a limited company to a public company in preparation for listing on the MAI Stock Exchange by changing its name from Nat Consulting Company Limited to Nat Absolute Technologies Public Company Limited on 1 June 2023.

Based on long experience as well as knowledge, awards, and executives' expertise Mr. Sutee Apichonratanakorn Chief Executive Officer (Founder) and Mr. Sahatat Trimetsoontorn Chief Operating Officer (Co-Founder), a modern executive with a vision to keep up and develop NATster within the organization. We are confident to lead and become a leader in technology with stability and sustainability.
