LET'S SAVE THE EARTH
25 Sep 2023

The greenhouse gases that envelop the Earth trap solar heat, leading to global warming and climate change. Currently, global warming is occurring at an unprecedented rate, surpassing any recorded in history. As temperatures continue to rise steadily over time, it disrupts and greatly impacts the balance of nature. This poses significant risks to humans and other living beings on Earth. The Earth's climate system is experiencing unprecedented changes over hundreds to thousands of years.

 

The climate change resulting from human activities affects every region, with increasing evidence of its linkage to severe heatwaves, heavy rainfall, droughts, and tropical storms. The Earth's surface temperature will continue to rise steadily, reaching at least a minimal increase of 1.5 degrees Celsius by mid-century if we do not reduce greenhouse gas emissions to the lowest appropriate level within a few decades. Beyond that, the impacts on the climate will intensify. We can mitigate global warming by significantly reducing cumulative carbon dioxide emissions and minimizing other greenhouse gases.

 

NAT Absolute Technologies Public Company Limited, has initiated a campaign to raise awareness among employees regarding the reduction of emissions into the environment. It begins with simple behavioral adjustments to reduce greenhouse gas emissions from various organizational activities. Our goal is to actively contribute to mitigating global warming for the sake of future generations.


Encouraging employees to be mindful of resource usage and act responsibly is vital. Here are ways to adjust behaviors to make efficient use of resources:

 

1. SAVE ELECTRICITY: Turn off lights when not in use

Encourage employees to turn off lights during periods of non-usage, such as during breaks, and only leave on the necessary lights that are in use.

These strategies promote a culture of resource conservation and responsibility among employees.


2. SAVE WATER: Turn off taps tightly every time

Employees are encouraged to turn off taps securely after use. The company has installed sensors to automatically shut off water taps when lights are turned off, preventing water wastage in case someone forgets to turn off the tap.

 By implementing these measures, we aim to maximize water conservation efficiency and reduce unnecessary water usage.



3. SAVE ENERGY: Turn off computer screens or set them to automatic shut down mode

The IT department provides guidance on screen saver settings and encourages employees to set a specific time for the screen to shut down when not in use. This initiative is part of the company's ongoing efforts to collect data and reduce energy consumption in various aspects, including fuel and other resources.

 

This strategy not only conserves energy but is also a step towards a more sustainable approach to resource usage.



"Source: Referenced from the findings of the study and projections from the 'Climate Change 2021: The Physical Science' report by IPCC (Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change)."
