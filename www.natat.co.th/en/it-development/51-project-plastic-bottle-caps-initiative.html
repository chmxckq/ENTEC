Project: Plastic Bottle Caps Initiative
1 Jul 2023

Environmental care is an integral part of the ESG (Environment, Social, Governance) policy.

It is a concept related to the sustainable development of an organization, with a focus on conducting business that considers responsibility in three main areas: environment, society, and governance.

Nat Absolute Technology Public Company Limited has organized activities to address environmental issues by encouraging employees to collect plastic bottle caps for donation to the Precious Plastic Bangkok project, which aims to recycle plastic waste.



     

Precious Plastic Bangkok is a project that focuses on utilizing unused plastic bottle caps to create new and beautiful products such as plates, bowls, plant pots, lamps, and decorative items. The income generated from selling these products will be used to establish recycling facilities for various communities.
